Miles Morales : SpiderMan

The Spider Verse Hero

Miles Morales represents a new generation of Spider-Man, blending Brooklyn roots with incredible spider-powered abilities. When he was bitten by a genetically altered spider, he gained amazing powers while learning that with great power comes great responsibility.

Spider teens

Miles and Gwen share a connection that transcends universes. As Spider-Man and Spider-Gwen, they understand the weight of the mask like no one else, balancing hero duties with the challenges of being extraordinary teenagers.
